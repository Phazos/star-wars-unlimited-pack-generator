Star Wars Unlimited Pack Generator

Before using this app, please organize your physical cards as follows:

  • Leaders: Place all your leader cards in one pile.
  • Bases: Place all your base cards in another pile.
  • Commons: Sort your common cards into five piles based on their aspect:
    • Red
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Yellow
    • Colorless
  • Uncommons: Sort your uncommon cards into five piles based on their aspect:
    • Red
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Yellow
    • Colorless
  • Rares/Legendaries: Combine all your rare and legendary cards, then sort them into five piles based on their aspect:
    • Red
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Yellow
    • Colorless

After sorting your cards, count the number of cards in each pile and enter those numbers into the corresponding input fields below. Then, specify the number of packs you want to generate (up to the maximum allowed based on your card counts). Click the "Generate Packs" button to create your packs. For each card in the generated pack, select the pile of the given rarity and aspect, then select the card from that pile based on the number given by counting that many cards into the pile.
